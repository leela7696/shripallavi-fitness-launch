

# Shripallavi Fitness & Gym — Marketing Website

## Overview
A modern, high-converting single-page marketing website with a dark premium theme (black + red accents), bold typography, smooth animations, and full mobile responsiveness. No backend needed — purely frontend.

---

## Design System
- **Theme**: Dark background (#0a0a0a / near-black) with vibrant red accents (#e11d48 or similar)
- **Typography**: Bold, strong headings with clean body text
- **Animations**: Smooth scroll-triggered fade-ins, subtle hover effects on cards/buttons
- **Layout**: Full-width sections with consistent spacing, fully responsive

---
